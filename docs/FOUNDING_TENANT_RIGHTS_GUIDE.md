### Rights Agreement Structure

#### Grant of Rights
```
N3XUS COS hereby grants [TENANT_NAME], 
as Founding Tenant #[ID], the following permanent, 
non-transferable, non-financial privileges...
```

#### Key Terms

1. **Perpetual Duration:** Rights last for platform lifetime
2. **Non-Transferable:** Rights die with account
3. **Non-Financial:** No equity or profit participation
4. **Service-Based:** Benefits are service access, not securities
5. **Revocable:** Only for severe ToS violations

#### Not a Security
- ‚ùå No investment of money

**No.** FTRs are permanently non-transferable.

**No.** Rights cannot be inherited or transferred.

**No.** They're service benefits, not financial instruments.

Only for severe ToS violations with appeals process.

### Do I have equity in the company?
**No.** FTRs grant no ownership or equity.

Original genesis phase participants only.

### Can more people become founding tenants?
**No.** Limited to original 13 participants.
