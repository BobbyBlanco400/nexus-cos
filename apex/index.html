FAQ

What is Nexus COS?
Nexus COS is an operating system for connected creative ecosystems. It provides a secure, observable core that unifies apps, services, and devices under intelligent routing and orchestration.
Is the Beta production‑grade?
Yes. The beta enforces strict HTTPS, validated health checks, and production-ready routing. All services are monitored and observable in real-time.
How do I start?
Click "Explore the Beta" to access the beta environment, or "Start Free" to create your account and begin building workflows.
