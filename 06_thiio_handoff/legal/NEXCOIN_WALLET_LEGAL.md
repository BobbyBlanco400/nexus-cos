# NexCoin Wallet - Legal Description

## Legal-Safe Wallet Description (Non-Public / Terms)

The NexCoin Wallet is a closed-loop platform credit system.

NexCoin has no cash value, is not a stored-value account, and is not redeemable for fiat currency.

All NexCoin balances represent access credits for digital services, entertainment, and platform features within Nexus COS. NexCoin does not represent an investment, security, or gambling custody account.

---

## Protection Coverage

This description protects against:
- **Payments Regulation**: Not a stored-value account or payment instrument
- **Gaming Regulation**: Not gambling custody or winnings
- **FinCEN Exposure**: Not a money service business
- **Jurisdictional Issues**: Platform credits, not currency
