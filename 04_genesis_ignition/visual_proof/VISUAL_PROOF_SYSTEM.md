## PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### What to Blur:
- ❌ Dollar amounts (revenue, profit)
- ❌ Customer information
- ❌ API keys, credentials, secrets
- ❌ Specific code logic
- ❌ Email addresses, contact info
- ❌ Invoice numbers, transaction IDs

### What to Keep Clear:
- ✅ Operating costs ($497 THIIO line item)
- ✅ Service provider logos (Stripe, THIIO)
- ✅ Evidence of activity (green indicators)
- ✅ File structure, project scale
- ✅ Professional workspace setup
- ✅ Timestamps (proves current)

### Visual Quality Standards:
1. **Professional** - Not amateur, not over-produced
2. **Clear** - Readable on mobile
3. **Authentic** - Real screenshots, not mockups
4. **Strategic blur** - Obvious but not suspicious
5. **No flex** - Authority, not showing off

---

## PSYCHOLOGICAL MECHANICS

### Why This Works:

1. **Proof Without Numbers** - Shows reality without revealing everything
2. **Activity Over Results** - "Work is happening" vs. "Look at our revenue"
3. **Operator Credibility** - Real operator, not marketer
4. **Mystery Maintained** - Enough proof to trust, not enough to fully understand
5. **Builder Recognition** - Builders see infrastructure, spectators see blur

### What NOT to Do:
- ❌ Show revenue numbers (comes off as flexing)
- ❌ Show user counts (comes off as bragging)
- ❌ Show growth charts (comes off as pitching)
- ❌ Over-explain (kills mystery)
- ❌ Under-blur (security risk)
