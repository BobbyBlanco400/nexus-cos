# DM CONVERSION ENGINE

**PURPOSE:** Single-path DM qualification and close system  
**OBJECTIVE:** Fewer conversations. Higher intent. Faster closes.  
**TARGET:** 20 Genesis Pass sales by 01/01/2026

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES

1. **Filter First** - Identify builders vs. spectators immediately
2. **Single Path** - One conversation flow, no branching
3. **Qualify Fast** - 3 messages to decision point
4. **Authority Tone** - We're not convincing, we're qualifying
5. **Close or Disqualify** - Every conversation ends in one or the other

## DM QUALIFICATION FLOW

### STEP 1: INITIAL CONTACT (They DM First)

**Typical Inbound Messages:**
- "Tell me more"
- "What is this?"
- "I'm interested"
- "How does this work?"
- "Is this legit?"

**Your Response (Copy-Paste Template):**

```
Genesis Pass is founder access to Nexus COS before public launch.

$500 one-time. 20 positions total. Closes 01/01/2026.

Quick question: Are you currently building something, or exploring opportunities?
```

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Gives minimal context (not full explanation)
- ✅ States facts (price, scarcity, deadline)
- ✅ Asks qualifying question immediately
- ✅ Separates builders from browsers

## RED FLAGS (DISQUALIFY IMMEDIATELY)

### Disqualify If They:
1. Ask for proof before committing
2. Want feature demos
3. Need testimonials
4. Request discounts or payment plans
5. Want to "think about it" for more than 48 hours
6. Ask if it's a scam multiple times
7. Compare to other platforms
8. Need their spouse/partner's approval

**These are spectators, not builders. Let them go.**

1. **After 3 explanations** - If they still don't get it, they won't

5. **After feature demo request** - Spectator, not builder

**Just say:**
```

```

**Then move on.**
